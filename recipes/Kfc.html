Welcome to KFC hot wings party!!!

About food :

KFC is an American fast food restaurant chain headquartered in Louisville, Kentucky, that specializes in fried chicken. It is the world's second-largest restaurant chain after McDonald's, with 22,621 locations globally in 150 countries as of December 2019.

Ingredients
  • chicken wings - 12 pcs.
    • FOR THE BREADING
  • eggs - 2.
  • milk - 1 cup (250 ml)
  • corn flour - 7 oz (200 g)
  • cornflakes - 5.5 oz (150 g)
  • chicken broth - 1/2 cube
    • SAUCE
  • mayonnaise - 3.5 oz (100 g)
  • ketchup - 3.5 oz (100 g)
  • mustard - 1/2 tsp.
  • black pepper
    HOW TO COOK:

  1. Wash the chicken wings and drain them of the water. Pour the corn flour into a bowl and mix it with the crumbled chicken broth cube.

  2. Pour the coarsely ground cornflakes in another container. In a 3rd container, beat the eggs and mix them with the milk.

  3. Dip the dried wings in the egg mixture, roll them in the flour, then dip in the eggs again and finally in the cornflakes. Fry the wings in a deep fryer.

  4. Serve the KFC-Style Chicken Wings with a sauce made from the mixed together ketchup, mayonnaise, mustard and black pepper.
